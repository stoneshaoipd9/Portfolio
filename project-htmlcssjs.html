Dental Office Website


(HTML Project: Wei Shao, Mike Yu, Laurian Staicu)


Description

The Dental Office Website includes total 16 web pages, one of them is the home page, six of them are service pages, six of them are the information pages, one of them is the blog page, one of them is the contact page, and the last one is the appointment page.
For the home page, the menu is on the top of the page, and it includes six main menus and twelve sub-menus; the main menu include home, service, info, blog, contact, and appointment menus, and service and info menus include six sub-menus respectively, Under the menu, there are three carousel slides; next, it is info section, and you can link to info pages; after that, it is service section, and you can link to service pages when you click the images; and next it is contact info area, the google map is on the left side, and the detail contact info is on the right side; before the footer, there are three buttons to link to the login, register, and appointment pages.
